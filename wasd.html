My name is David Binetti, and I'm honored to serve as Trustee for the West Ada School District. I represent Zone 4, which comprises the northern portion of the District, including Eagle, Star, and parts of Meridian and Garden City.

I'm always eager to hear from those I serve, so please do not hesitate to reach out to me either by email at dbinetti@gmail.com, or via phone/text at 208.713.4599.

You can also stay in touch by subscribing to my WASD newsletter where I keep patrons updated on current District policies. I'm required to point out that these respresent my own personal thoughts, and do reflect the position of the District, Administration, or any other Board Member. Still, people find them a valuable and consise way to stay connected.
